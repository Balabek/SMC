Program Overview

This Course is for Business professionals who start from scratch or with very basic blockchain knowledge and who wish to learn the mechanics and disruptive opportunities at the monetary, technological and investment levels that present themselves now and in the future in the world of blockchain and cryptoeconomics.

Objectives

The coursework prepares you not as a specialist in one functional area of blockchain, but prepares you to be a highly qualified professional equipped with multidimensional skillset. The curriculum focuses on several blockchain topics with focus on Crypto Economy Foundation, Projects and ICO’s, Law and Regulations and Crypto Trading while emphasizing leadership, and strategic thinking across all units.

Masterclass Outline & Sequence

Intro to Blockchain Fundamentals

This introduction unit gives you fundamentals that makes up a Blockchain and introduces to the basic concepts. You will also get an overview over the milestones in Blockchain history.

Blockchain & Crypto Currency Economy Foundation

This unit is aimed at anyone who wants to start and acquire basic and intermediate knowledge to understand the world of cryptocurrencies, tokens, ICOs, smart contracts and the different types of blockchain applications.

You will learn how Bitcoin works, about the concept of Double Spending and other characteristics of cryptocurrency and the unit presents you real-life case studies across multiple industries and sectors

Benefits of a Blockchain

This unit focuses on benefits and drawbacks when using blockchain technology. The advantages like decentralization, security and tokenization are evaluated against efficiency, performance and scalability issues and much more.

Decentralized Networks

In this unit students will learn how a decentralized network is useful to create trust in its data and how it is immune against denial of service attacks and other issues.

Blockchain Types

This unit explains the different types of blockchains like Public/Private, Open/Closed andPermissioned/Permissionless. The completion of this unit helps on the decision about your blockchain solution.

Creation of a Block

This unit focuses on the creation of clocks, the concept of consensus and how blocks are chained together.

Hashing

This unit describes applied hashing in blockchain technology. The student will learn about Merkle Trees and their concept of efficient data validation.

Mining

In this unit the student will learn how Mining of a block works and how a blockchain develops. The mining of bitcoins is used as an example here.

Consensus

Byzantine Fault Tolerance, Proof of Work, Proof of Stake and other Consensus Mechanisms are the topics of this unit. Students will experience the different types of consensus as principles of cryptocurrencies and other blockchain applications.

Ethereum & Blockchain 2.0

This unit shows how Ethereum introduced the concept of “blockchain 2.0” platforms by introducing the concept of Smart Contracts. Students will learn, why Smart Contracts provide autonomy, backup, efficiency, accuracy and saving costs.

Applied Blockchain

This unit gives insight into application of blockchain in the fields of:

  • Personal Background Checks
  • Personal Identification
  • Land Registries
  • Financial Services
  • Global Supply Chain
  • Healthcare
  • Airlines
  • Tokenized Economies
  • Payment Channels

New Business Models

Blockchain opens up entirely new business models, due to the fact blockchain is able to transcend physical and geographical barriers and uses math and cryptography to enable transactions globally. This Unit shows how.

Web 3.0

This unit introduces the Internet of Value. It shows how Web 3.0 plans to decentralize finance.

Blockchain Pros & Cons

This unit focuses on the situation of blockchain in real life. It helps you to decide if blockchain is always the right solution for your database projects. It concludes your knowledge about blockchain and qualifies you to advise on blockchain projects.
