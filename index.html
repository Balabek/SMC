FAQ

Is SMC an accredited Higher Education Institution?

Yes. SMC is institutionally accredited by the National Commission for Further and Higher Education of the Ministry of Education.

Which programs of SMC are accredited?

All Degree Programs and Diploma Programs are accredited by the National Commission for Further and Higher Education.

Are the Joint Programs offered by SMC accredited?

The joint programs are operated by SMC and not covered by the NCFHE. The degrees of the Joint Programs are conferred by our academic partner UCN, which is a leading government-accredited University in the Americas.

How much tuition do I have to pay?

The tuition depends on your choice of program. Details are highlighted in each program section of the website.

Which payment options do you I have?

You may choose between a discounted upfront payment or a flexible payment schedule. Individual payment schedules are available upon request. We do accept bank transfer and credit card payments

Can I get financial assistance from SMC?

We do provide financial assistance upon request and documentation. Due to current global events like the Pandemic, we do grant allowances of up to 25% and interest free down payments in a straight-forward process upon request.

How do I attend classes at SMC?

All you need in order to study at SMC is a reliable internet connection. The best experience can be achieved with a standard Laptop, Notebook, or PC. You can also have a great study experience on a Tablet, and even a Smart Phone.

Is physical attendance required to study at SMC?

No physical attendance is needed. The distance learning programs offered by SMC are 100% online via our state-of-the-art eLearning system.

Can I study on-site at a SMC Campus?

SMC does not offer any on-site programs.

Are international students accepted at SMC?

Students from all over the world may join SMC and attend our programs. Since 100% online you may study from any place, country, or region. All you need is an internet connection.
